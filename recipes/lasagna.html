Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish originated from Italy and is popular all over the world.

It's an easy to make, yet delicious dish that can be compared to pasta bolognese.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasoning
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
